Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fulora?
Fulora is your personal reading companion for iOS. It helps you build consistent reading habits, track your progress, organize your reading materials, and sync your library across devices. Whether you're reading PDFs, books, or documents, Fulora makes it easy to stay on top of your reading goals.
How do I import books or PDFs?
You can import files in several ways:
  • Tap the "+" button in your library
  • Use the iOS Share Sheet from other apps
  • Import from Files app
  • Open files directly from email or cloud storage
How does reading progress tracking work?
Fulora automatically tracks your reading progress as you read. You can view your daily reading time, pages read, and maintain reading streaks. Your progress syncs across devices when you're signed in.
What are Collections?
Collections allow you to organize your books into custom categories. You can create collections for different topics, reading goals, or any way you prefer to organize your library. Books can be added to multiple collections.
How do I sync my library across devices?
Sign in with your Google account to enable cloud sync. Your library, collections, bookmarks, and reading progress will automatically sync across all your devices where you're signed in.
Can I annotate and highlight in PDFs?
Yes! Fulora supports PDF annotations including highlighting, underlining, and drawing with Apple Pencil. Your annotations are saved and synced across devices.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes, your privacy is our priority. Your reading data is stored securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
What file formats does Fulora support?
Fulora currently supports PDF files. Support for additional formats is planned for future updates.
