Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Welcome to Fulora. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our reading companion app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Google or Apple Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile information provided by these services.
  • Reading Data: Your reading progress, annotations, highlights, and bookmarks.
  • Documents: PDF files and reading materials you upload to the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including reading habits and preferences.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Fulora service
  • Sync your reading progress across devices through Fulora Cloud
  • Personalize your reading experience
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Send you important updates about the service
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Fulora Cloud

Fulora Cloud securely stores your reading progress and documents in the cloud. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Fulora is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.
